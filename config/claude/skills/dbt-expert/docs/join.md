Want to learn how organizations around the world are tackling the biggest challenges in data while making new friends from the best analytics teams? Join the dbt Community — data practitioners’ favorite place to learn new skills, keep on top of industry trends, and forge connections.

[![](https://docs.getdbt.com/img/icons/slack.svg)

#### Join us on Slack

Follow the pulse of the dbt Community! Chat with other practitioners in your city, country or worldwide about data work, tech stacks, or simply share a killer meme.](https://www.getdbt.com/community/join-the-community/)

[![](https://docs.getdbt.com/img/icons/discussions.svg)

#### Community Forum

Have a question about how to do something in dbt? Hop into the Community Forum and work with others to create long lived community knowledge.](/community/forum)

[![](https://docs.getdbt.com/img/icons/pencil-paper.svg)

#### How to contribute

Want to get involved? This is the place! Learn how to contribute to our public repositories, write for the blog, speak at a meetup and more.](/community/contribute)

[![](https://docs.getdbt.com/img/icons/folder.svg)

#### Code of Conduct

We are committed to creating a space where everyone can feel welcome and safe. Our Code of Conduct reflects the agreement that all Community members make to uphold these ideals.](/community/resources/code-of-conduct)

[![](https://docs.getdbt.com/img/icons/calendar.svg)

#### Upcoming events

Whether it's in-person Meetups in your local area, Coalesce – the annual Analytics Engineering Conference – or online Office Hours there are options for everyone.](/community/events)

[![](https://docs.getdbt.com/img/icons/star.svg)

#### Watch past events

Get a taste for the energy of our live events, get inspired, or prepare for an upcoming event by watching recordings from our YouTube archives.](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL0QYlrC86xQl1DGKBopQZiZ6tSqrMlD2M)
