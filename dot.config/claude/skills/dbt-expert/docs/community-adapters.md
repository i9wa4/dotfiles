We welcome and encourage [adapter plugins contributions](https://docs.getdbt.com/docs/contribute-core-adapters#contribute-to-a-pre-existing-adapter) from the dbt community. Please be mindful that these [community maintainers](https://docs.getdbt.com/docs/connect-adapters#maintainers) are intrepid volunteers who donate their time and effort â€” so be kind, understanding, and help out where you can!
